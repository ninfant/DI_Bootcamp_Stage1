Céline Dion has received numerous awards and accolades throughout her career, including many Grammy Awards, American Music Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and Juno Awards, among others. Some of her most notable awards are:

Grammy Awards

She has won 5 Grammy Awards, including the prestigious "Album of the Year" for "Falling Into You" (1997) and "Best Female Vocal Performance" for "My Heart Will Go On" (1999).

Billboard Music Awards

She has received multiple Billboard awards, including recognitions for "Top Selling Artist" and "Top Adult Contemporary Artist".

American Music Awards

She has been honored with more than 20 American Music Awards, including "Artist of the Year" and "Song of the Year".

Juno Awards

Céline Dion has won over 20 Juno Awards, which are the most prestigious music awards in Canada.

César Awards

She has won the "César Award" for "Best Original Song" for the Titanic movie soundtrack (1997).
